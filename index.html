Let's Make Hummus

Wow I love hummus.

Ingredients

15 oz canned chickpeas
1/4 cup fresh lemon juice (1 large lemon)
1/4 cup well-stirred tahini
1 small garlic clove, minced
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
2 to 3 tablespoons water
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
Dash of ground paprika
Salt to taste

Directions

1. Gather your ingredients.
2. Blend chickpeas, lemon juice, tahini, garlic, olive oil, cumin and salt in a food processor until smooth.
3. Stream reserved chickpea liquid into the mixture as it blends until desired consistency is achieved.
4. Taste and adjust seasonings, adding more salt, lemon juice or cumin as needed.
